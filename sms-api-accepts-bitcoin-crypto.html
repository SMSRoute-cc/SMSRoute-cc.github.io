Immediately. Once the crypto network confirms your transaction, your SMSRoute balance is updated automatically. You can then use your API key to send messages — no waiting for manual approval.

Is there a minimum amount I must spend per month?

Does SMSRoute work for sending SMS to India without DLT registration?

SMSRoute supports sending to India, but Indian telecom regulations require DLT registration for all senders. SMSRoute can help you navigate the process, but you must register your entity with TRAI. The advantage is that SMSRoute does not require any additional KYC beyond what DLT mandates.

Can I get a refund for unused crypto balance?

Yes. SMSRoute will refund your unused balance to the originating cryptocurrency wallet on request. There are no hidden fees or penalties.
