SMSRoute is the SMS marketing platform without KYC that lets you send bulk SMS to your own opt-in lists with no identity verification, no business documents, and no waiting. Sign up with just an email, fund with crypto (BTC, ETH, USDT, XMR, LTC, SOL), and start sending via API or dashboard in minutes — no vetting, no contracts, no monthly minimums.

What does “no KYC” mean for SMS marketing?

KYC (Know Your Customer) is the process where providers demand government ID, business registration, proof of address, and sometimes a video call before you can send a single message. SMSRoute skips all of that. You sign up with an email address, top up with cryptocurrency, and your API key is active immediately. There are no identity documents to upload, no verification delays, and no one asking what your business does. This is ideal for marketers, developers, and individuals who already have a clean, opt-in list and want to message it without bureaucratic friction.

How does SMSRoute compare to Twilio, Vonage, or Plivo for no-KYC marketing?

Twilio, Vonage, and Plivo all require identity verification, business registration, and often additional vetting for A2P 10DLC (US) or DLT (India). They also mandate card or invoice billing, retain your customer data, and charge higher rates — Twilio Verify starts at $0.05 per verification. SMSRoute requires none of that. You get a no-KYC SMS API with crypto billing, custom sender names on request, and built-in STOP-keyword handling. The trade-off is that SMSRoute is designed for responsible marketers messaging their own lists, not for cold spam. We enforce opt-in consent and provide suppression lists to keep your sending clean.

What are the pricing and delivery details?

We cover 149 countries with adaptive multi-route delivery and automatic failover. Failed or undelivered messages are auto-credited back to your balance, and unused balance is refundable to your originating wallet on request. You also get free test credits on signup. For full pricing, see our prices page.

Can I use a custom sender name (alphanumeric sender ID)?

Yes. By default, SMSRoute assigns a smart shared sender pool for high deliverability. If your target routes support custom alphanumeric sender IDs, you can request one — no extra verification needed. Inbound numbers are also available on request. This gives you branding control without the KYC hurdles that other providers impose.

How do I send bulk SMS via API without verification?

After signing up with your email, fund your account with BTC, ETH, USDT (TRC-20/ERC-20), XMR, LTC, or SOL. Top-ups confirm automatically. Then generate an API key from the dashboard. You can send via REST API or SMPP with code examples in Python, PHP, Go, and Node.js (see GitHub examples). The platform includes scheduling, bulk sending, link shortening, real-time DLR webhooks, and a dashboard for manual sends. No contracts, no monthly minimums.

Is SMSRoute compliant with anti-spam regulations?

Absolutely. SMSRoute is built for opt-in consent only. We provide built-in STOP-keyword handling and suppression lists so you can manage unsubscribes automatically. We do not support cold spam or purchased lists. While SMS as a protocol lacks end-to-end encryption, SMSRoute does not retain your message content after delivery, and because we have no KYC, there is nothing to leak. This is a responsible framing: you message your own lists, we handle the infrastructure, and everyone stays compliant.

What support and reliability can I expect?

SMSRoute offers 24/7 support via Telegram and email, with typical first response under an hour. Our platform has maintained 99.9%+ uptime over the trailing twelve months. We provide real-time DLR webhooks, a status page at smsroute.cc/status/, and automatic failover across multiple routes. If a message fails to deliver, your balance is credited back automatically.
