International route vs. domestic DLT route – what’s the tradeoff?

India’s TRAI DLT (Distributed Ledger Technology) rules require all domestic SMS senders to register their sender IDs, templates, and headers. This process can take days or weeks, and non-compliance can block messages. SMSRoute’s international route does not require DLT registration – messages are delivered via global carrier agreements that bypass the domestic DLT framework.

The tradeoff: international routes may have slightly higher latency (still under 5 seconds typically). But for most bulk use cases – OTPs, alerts, marketing – delivery is reliable and fast. If you need DLT-compliant domestic routing, SMSRoute can support that on request for custom setups. For a full explanation of DLT categories and compliance, see our TRAI DLT category guide.
